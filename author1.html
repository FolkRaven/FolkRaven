About Me

Some of my earliest memories are of hunting for bugs in the garden while birds sang in the bushes. I was overjoyed every time I found a wiggling worm, a busy beetle, or even a slimy slug. I had several pet snails over the years, but they were always escaping the small houses I built for them in the garden!

My school years were largely spent daydreaming and doodling - much to my teachers’ frustration I’m sure - and I was forever making small books out of scrap of paper. Becoming an author (no matter how cliche it sounds) has always been my dream job.

When my children were younger, we enjoyed creating stories together. Soon I was writing and illustrating short stories at home to help them learn how to read and count. Which brings us back to bugs.

The first version of Count with me: 1-10 Bug fun is the very book that helped my son learn how to count to 10!

And so, together with Catherine, copious amounts of coffee, and years of hard work, FolkRaven was born.

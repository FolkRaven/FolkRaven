About this book

🩺 Doctor Penny
A Heartwarming Tale of Make-Believe, Cuddles, and Cups of Tea

Step into the world of Doctor Penny, where kindness, imagination, and a touch of pretend medicine come together in the coziest way.

When her cuddly patients need a helping hand, little Penny knows just what to do. 

This delightful rhyming story celebrates the magic of pretend play, the comfort of caring, and the joy of slowing down for a well-deserved rest.

Whether it's a pretend bandage or a warm cuddle, Doctor Penny reminds us all that sometimes, a little imagination and a nice cup of tea make everything better.

☕ Why Families Love Doctor Penny:

  • 🧸 Encourages Imaginative Play – A relatable story for any child who’s played “doctor” with their toys.
  • 💕 Themes of Care & Empathy – Gentle lessons in kindness and looking after others.
  • 🎶 Rhythmic, Repetitive Text – Perfect for reading aloud and easy for children to join in.
  • 🌙 A Calming Bedtime Wind-Down – Ends with a cozy moment between Penny and her mum, making it an ideal story for winding down at the end of the day.
